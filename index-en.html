Best mobile App

The Zozonoza App serves to sensitize and thus to prevent the spread of the zozonoza virus in the population as far as possible. Users of the app record their encounters using digital handshakes. If one of the persons reports sick, all persons stored as contacted in the last two days are informed. In addition, the app contains further information material on the Zozonoza virus.
